Bio

Qiangguo Jin is currently an associate professor@Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), China. His research lies in medical image analysis and bioinformatics. Qiangguo Jin has published papers in highly regarded journals and conferences such as KNOSYS, ASOC, MICCAI.

Research Interests

Medical Image Analysis: Mainly focusing on improving lesion detection and anatomical structure analysis.
Bioinformatics: Mainly focusing on drug development, complex disease diagnosis and treatment.

Our group has several Master positions. If you are a self-motivated student with proficient programming skills and strong interests in the research directions, please send me your CV.
